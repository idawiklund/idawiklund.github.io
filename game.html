Anti-Tower Defence Game


A project where me and three others worked together and developed an "Anti-Tower Defense" game. A game where you send out the troops and the game try to shoot them down with its towers. The game is completely made up in Java, with home made images in Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.

When playing the game, the goal is to get a set number of troops from the start, across the playing field and safely to the goal. This while towers are shooting laser at the troops! You get an amount of credits to buy troops for. They have slightly different skill sets. See the video for an example of how to play the game.
