Designing the course report system


A project where me and two other students worked together and made a design proposal for the course report system at Umeå University. The prototypes are made up in the Adobe programs Photoshop, Illustrator and XD.

The video shows a part of our design for the course report system. We demonstrated this prototype and the rest of our design proposal for the ones responsible for the system, ITS and the Technical Faculty of Science. They were about to revise their system and got inspiration from all students from me and my class.
